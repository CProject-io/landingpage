Our Vision

C-Project is a sustainable and autonomous digital infrastructure ecosystem. Its core innovation is 'Proof-of-Generation' (PoG), a consensus mechanism where network security is based on the verifiable generation of renewable energy, rather than its consumption. This is achieved through a network of independent physical nodes, each powered by its own solar or wind energy system.

The ultimate goal is not to replace internet providers, but to build a free and universal access infrastructure layer (Layer 0). This network aims to ensure that anyone, anywhere, can access basic and essential services, such as making a DeFi transaction or sending a P2P message, in a resilient and censorship-resistant manner.

In essence, C-Project creates a virtuous cycle: clean energy keeps the network alive, and this network, in turn, provides fundamental digital access, backing its token's value on a real and tangible productive process.

Core Innovation: Proof-of-Generation

PoG changes the game. Instead of burning energy to secure a blockchain, ours grows stronger with every watt of clean energy generated by its community.
