The C-Project Blockchain

The logical and economic heart of our ecosystem, built to be flexible, sustainable, and secure.

Tech Stack: Polkadot / Substrate

Our blockchain is built using **Substrate**, the development framework from Polkadot. We chose Substrate for its incredible modularity, security, and customization capabilities. It allows us to "unplug" standard consensus mechanisms and "plug in" our own Proof-of-Generation without having to reinvent the entire blockchain technology stack.

Consensus: Proof-of-Generation (PoG)

PoG is our greatest innovation. Instead of PoW or PoS, block validation and network security depend on the proof of renewable energy generation. This not only solves the energy consumption problem but also anchors the ecosystem's value to a real and beneficial productive process. For more details, see our PoG documentation.

Modular Logic: Pallets

Thanks to Substrate, all of C-Project's business logic is organized into modules called **pallets**. We have dedicated pallets for:

  • PoG: Manages energy reporting and validation.
  • Tokenomics: Controls the issuance and distribution of rewards.
  • Governance: Allows the community to make decisions about the project's future.
  • Use Cases: Specific pallets for services like messaging or payments.

Tokenomics: Intrinsic Value

C-Project's native token is designed to have intrinsic value. Part of its valuation is linked to the real cost of producing the renewable energy that the network generates. This creates a value "floor" and differentiates it from purely speculative assets, fostering a more stable and predictable digital economy.
